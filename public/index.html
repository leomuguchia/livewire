About us

Livewire Mashariki Studio Limited or better known as Livewire Mashariki Africa, is dynamic organizations that are committed to youth empowerment, media production, and advocating for human rights.Our names are inspired by the combination of “Livewire,” symbolizing energy, vibrancy, and connection, and “Mashariki,” which means “East” in Swahili, representing our East African roots and focus of a new dawn in Africa
